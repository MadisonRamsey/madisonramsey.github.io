NYC Citi Bike Metrics

As the culminating project for my Applied Machine Learning course, I worked with two classmates to apply machine learning techniques to 2019 New York City bike share data (n=17,021,558) to determine what factors are likely to influence bike demand in the Citi Bike system and better understand how the company can approach bike reallocation across its 750 docking stations. We evaluate total hourly bike demand across the entire Citi Bike system, hourly incoming and outgoing bike demand at each docking station, and various metrics corresponding to individual rides.
